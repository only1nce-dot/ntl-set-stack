| Constraint | Foundation | Growth | Storm |

| Max Total Allocated | $12,000 (50%) | $8,400 (35%) | $3,600 (15%) |
| Max Single Position | 20% of bucket | 15% of bucket | 30% of bucket |
| Max Daily Risk | 0.5% of portfolio | 1% of portfolio | 1.5% of portfolio |
| Stop Loss | -15% of position | -20% of position | -30% of position |
| Rebalance Threshold | ±10% | ±15% | ±20% |

### Portfolio-Level Constraints

- **Max Total Risk:** 2% of portfolio per trade (global constraint)
- **Max Active Positions:** 3 positions open simultaneously (any bucket)
- **Max Capital Deployed:** 40% of cash at any time
- **Global Stop Loss:** -10% of total portfolio (emergency protection)

### Rebalancing Rules

1. **Monthly Rebalancing:** Check bucket allocations on 1st of each month
2. **Quarterly Deep Rebalancing:** Full portfolio review and rebalance using EOD global audit
3. **Trigger-Based Rebalancing:**
   - Any bucket moves >10% from target allocation → rebalance
   - Any single position moves >15% from target weight → rebalance
   - Market correction >20% → review all positions
4. **Rebalancing Method:** Use cash buffer first, then trim over-weighted positions
